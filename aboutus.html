About Us

Welcome to SuperLike! We are a leading platform bridging the gap between construction companies and clients seeking top-notch services in Cape Town and Johannesburg. Our mission is simple: to connect skilled construction professionals with potential customers who need their expertise. We generate high-quality leads and assign them to the right construction companies in the relevant areas.

What sets us apart? Joining SuperLike is completely free for construction companies. There are no upfront costs or setup fees—just a streamlined process that allows you to focus on what you do best: delivering exceptional construction services. We operate on a commission basis, taking a small percentage from the quote provided to the customer for each job we bring you. This means you only pay when you succeed, making our model both efficient and cost-effective.

With SuperLike, you gain access to a steady stream of job opportunities without the hassle of marketing and lead generation. Our platform ensures that you connect with clients who are actively seeking your services, helping you grow your business and take on more projects.

Join us today and experience the benefits of a partnership designed to enhance your business while keeping costs low. At SuperLike, we’re here to support your success every step of the way.
